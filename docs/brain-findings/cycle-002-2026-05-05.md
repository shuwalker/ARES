### Key Insight
**HASEL/electrohydraulic actuators are the quiet actuation frontier.** They're inherently silent (no gears, no motors in the traditional sense), compliant by nature, and the research is accelerating (3 papers in Q1 2026 alone). The catch: control is nonlinear and complex. But for a character robotics application where perfect precision isn't required (unlike surgery), HASEL actuators could be a differentiator. No competitor is using them in consumer/character robotics.
